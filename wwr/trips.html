What Trips Do We Offer?

The photo to the left is depicting the Snake River, our most popular rafting site. The Snake River is ling, winding, wild, and beautiful. You will raft thorugh the mountains of Idaho for a total of 4 miles in 45 minutes.

The picture to the right shows a photo taken of one of our rafters going through the salt river rapids. The salt river is our most rigorous rafting trip, taking people through the swiftest rapids around. The trip will take 45 minutes to complete and will cover a total of 5 miles.

This is a photo taken at our beginner rafting site, New Creek. New Creek is a wonderful, calm rafting experience to help new rafters acclamate to the new hobby. There are a few swift rapids, but the river mostly remains calm, allowing you to travel 2 miles over the span of 25 minutes.
